About Humming bird

Hummingbird provides a fast way of assessing risk to Covid-19. HummingBird allows users to input their symptoms, history, and other factors and in return receive an assessment of the likelihood of having COVID -19. Hummingbird will also provide users with an assessment of infection severity as well as information on getting officially tested and treated. In addition we will be focusing on using information from the greater Los Angeles area.
