Sympotoms of Covid

COVID-19 symptoms can range from mild to severe and may include fever, cough, shortness of breath, loss of taste or smell, fatigue, and body aches.

  • Fever or Chills
  • Cough
  • Shortness of breath or diffuculty breathing
  • Fatigue
  • Muscle or body aches
  • Headache
  • New loss of taste or smell
  • Sore throat
  • Congestion or runny nose
  • Nausea or vomitting
  • Diarrhea

The following list only shows a number of symptoms It is important to keep in mind that people with underlying conditions like heart or lung disease are at a higher risk for getting very sick from COVID - 19.
